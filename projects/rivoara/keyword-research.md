# Rivoara Keyword Research - Deep Dive
*Updated: 2026-01-27*

## Pain Point Language (German)

Real words people use when complaining about hard water + hair:

### Primary Complaints
- "Haare von Tag zu Tag immer kaputter und trockener" (hair getting more damaged and dry day by day)
- "stark abbrechen" (breaking badly)
- "glanzlos und strohig" (dull and straw-like)
- "strohige Haare" / "Strohspitzen" (straw-like hair / straw tips)
- "kaputte Haare" (damaged hair)
- "Haare nicht richtig ausspülen kann" (can't rinse hair properly)
- "Shampoo-/Duschgelfilm bleibt zurück" (shampoo/gel film stays behind)
- "Haare fühlen sich stumpf und trocken an" (hair feels dull and dry)
- "nimmt den Glanz" (takes away the shine)

### Trigger Moments
- "Wir sind umgezogen" (we moved) — people discover problem after moving
- Berlin, Frankfurt mentioned frequently — known hard water cities
- "hartes Wasser in der Leitung" (hard water in pipes)

## Content Angles That Work
1. **Educational** — "Warum redet keiner über..." (why does nobody talk about...)
2. **Before/After** — transformation content
3. **City-specific** — Berlin, Frankfurt, München hard water
4. **Expat angle** — English content for newcomers to Germany
5. **Product tests** — reviewing shower filters

## Audience Segments
1. **Movers** — just moved to hard water area, noticing damage
2. **Expats** — international people in German cities
3. **Hair-conscious** — people already invested in haircare
4. **Problem solvers** — actively searching for solutions

## Messaging Implications for Rivoara
- Lead with **outcomes** (glänzende, gesunde Haare) not mechanism
- Address the **move trigger** — "Neue Stadt, neue Probleme?"
- Position against **DIY solutions** (vinegar rinse = messy, inconsistent)
- Target **Berlin/Frankfurt/München** specifically
- Use **before/after** format for social proof
